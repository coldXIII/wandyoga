About Us

Welcome to Wand Yoga Studio

We offer over 4000 professionaly filmed online yoga classes taught by experienced, certified yoga teachers to customers around the world. We believe that yoga should be accessible to everyone and our classes are designed to give you an in-studio experience, at an affordable price, in your own home.


Wand yoga is designed and created specificially to serve you. Our goal is to create a destination for people of all experience levels and backgrounds to practice, grow and heal. Our mission is to create a community of health and wellbeing through the practice of yoga. Wand Yoga is a space that will inspire growth and self-awerness. Our passion is to motivate one`s desire to achieve their full potential. Wand Yoga will welcome and encourage your every step on the way.
